Tabakhmaaz

Tabak maaz a traditional meat product of wazwan made up of rib portion of lamb/mutton meat

It is one of many dishes that is present on the plate being served which is known as trami in the traditional language. The rib portion of the lamb meat is fried in desi ghee and the key ingredients added while cooking are cardamom,fennel and dried ginger. It is very crispy and crunchy to eat.
