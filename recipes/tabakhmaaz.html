Tabakhmaaz

Tabak maaz-a traditional meat product of wazwan made up of rib portion of lamb/mutton meat

It is one of many dishes that is present on the plate being served which is known as trami in the traditional language. It is very crispy and crunchy to eat.

The key ingredients required for making this dish are:

  • Rib portion of sheep.
  • Desi Ghee.
  • Salt.
  • Turmeric.
  • Ground Garlic.
  • Dry Ginger Powder.
  • Cloves.
  • Black Cardamom.
